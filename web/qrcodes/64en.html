Pushkin's memorial estate (Vyazemy estate)

Tombstone of unknown grave in the churchyard

Is a massive white-stone tombstone of a rectangular shape. The front surface is decorated with ornaments typical of the late XVI - XVII centuries. Ancient epitaph have been preserved to present day. It is the only gravestone of Godunov time on the territory of Transfiguration (Trinity) church.

Pushkin's memorial estate (Vyazemy estate)

Godunov's Ravine

Located in the northern part of the estate complex, between the Smolenskoe (Mozhayskoe) highway and the the center of the estate. Ravine started from ""Ramenkoe pond"" and ended with Vyazemka river. Wet land of the river bed which dries up every summer have been preserved to present day. It is an integral part of the park complex, a monument of the historic landscape.

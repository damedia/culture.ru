The memorial estate Arkhangelskoye

"Grief"

"Grief" - sculpture made by bronze (by Bart KG) - a figure of youth with a fading torch was set by Zinaida Nikolaevna Yusupova - as a sign of mourning for the deceased in a duel eldest son Nicholas. The sculpture was installed in the beginning of XX century on the estate "Arkhangelskoye"

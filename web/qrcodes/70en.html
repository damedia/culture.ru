Pushkin's memorial estate (Zakharovo estate)

The main house.

On the 200th anniversary of the birth of the poet main house was restored upon the project by N.E.Kartashova and V.V.Zubarev on funds of Odintsovo district. It is wooden two-story building with a second floor mezzanine, facade, columns and belvedere with enfilade arrangement of rooms. The estate with the house was biught by the grandmother of the poet Mary Hannibal in 1804 and she ran it until 1810. Alexander Pushkin spent here his childhood.

Pushkin's memorial estate (Vyazemy estate)

Vyazemka river.

A small affluent of the Moscow River, its length is nearly 21-23 km. The river has two sources - Big and Small Vyazemka. The river valley is clearly formed, almost all of her upper stream turned into a continuous cascade of ponds regulated by dams. Mouth of the river is near the Gorki-X village, sources are near Butyni village.

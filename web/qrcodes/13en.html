The memorial estate Abramtsevo

Polenov family's country house

The building was built in 1882 by local workers for a young married couple Vasily and Natalia Polenov. The house was heated by the stove and fireplace, tiled with tiles. Interior design was very simple - wooden floor and walls, the furniture also ws very simple - a table, a bench and a case. The house served to Polenov as a workshop, venue for domestic performances of the Abramtsevo artistic circle. In the early twentieth century Pyotr Konchalovsky, M. Nesterov, Viktor Vasnetsov, writer L.Leonov stayed here in summer in Abramtsevo. In 1915-1916 here was the hospital, and later - living room and dining room of the sanatorium located on the estate. Today this house hosts exhibitions.

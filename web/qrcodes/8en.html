The memorial estate Abramtsevo

The kitchen

The building was constructed in 1870 on the site of an old Aksakov kitchen. In May-July 1870 Mamontov family ived here, they moved to Abramtsevo while the estait was reconstructed. Later, a kitchen used as intended, it also had maid's room. The oven was in the center of the building. The Kitchen is a typical house of the Central Russia. The interior consisted of the kitchen and servant's rooms. In 1881 a storage room was constructed in the northern side of the building. In the late XIX - early XX century. there was a passageway connecting the main house with the kitchen. At present, the kitchen costs the collection of Russian folk art, gathered by the participants of Abramtsevo artistic circle and additions of the XX century. Here are the furniture,domestic and farm utensils and etc.

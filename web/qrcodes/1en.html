The memorial estate Abramtsevo

Banya

It was built in 1877-1878 by architect I. Ropet (real name Ivan Petrov, 1844-1908), it replace the old bany by Aksakov. It is one of the monuments of the""Russian style"". It is a wooden house with ""a checkered"" ornament. Decorative porch of the southern facade is toppedwith wooden ""kokoshnik"" richly decorated with carvings. Small windows with massive roof and small doors gives it fantastical appearance, for which it got its second name — ""The Mansion."" Banya was hardly used, and has been a guest wing. Here in 1910-1911 stayed P. Konchalovsky, in 1920 — artist A. Vishnecsky also came here. At present, the building is an exhibition hall where one can see Abramtsevo carpentry workshop's exhibition. Here art the furniture and household items made upon E. Polenova's sketches.

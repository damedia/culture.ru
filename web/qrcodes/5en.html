The memorial estate Abramtsevo

The Oak

The oak (Latin Quércus róbur). One of the oldest trees of the estate, the age is more than 300 years. The circumference of the barrel is more than 4.5 meters. It is a live contemporary and witness to many historical events that took place in Abramtsevo.

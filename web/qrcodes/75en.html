Pushkin's memorial estate (Zakharovo estate)

Zakharovsky pond.

Previous owners of the Zakharovo estate constructed the dam, which created the pond. Near the old pond - Pushkin's ""mirror of water"" – the new pond was digged. Sharapovka and Ploschanka rivers disembogues their waters into the pond. And hear in the pond Sharapovka river which disembogues its waters into the Viazemka river starts. Some of the trees on the bank of the pond were planted in Pushkin's time.

Pushkin's memorial estate (Vyazemy estate)

Osorgin family's tombstone in the churchyard

Classic tombstone of gray granite. It is a stylized urn on a pedestal. One can read the epitaph on the top of the monument,: "Under this stone is body of the Lieutenant Colonel Gerasim G. Osorgin. Who died at 6 o'clock 24 August 1802. He lived a life of 71 years 5 months and 19 days", "Ekaterina Mikhailovna Osorgin. Died at 6 o'clock 12 March 1825. She lived 74 years 4 months and 14 days", "Here lie the remains of a baby Gerasim Mikhailovich Osorgin. 21 January 1826 - 16 February 1826" G. Osorgin - was public figure of Catherine's time. He participated in suppressing of Pugachev rebellion. The monument was brought hear from an abandoned cemetery near Zvenigorod in 2010.

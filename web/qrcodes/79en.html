Pushkin's memorial estate (Zakharovo estate)

Pushkinskaya birch.

On one of the birch trees in the park in Zakharovskaya. In 1851 N.Berg found several incised lines of poetry on it. Perhaps they could belong to the young poet. In 2011, Birch was struck by lightning, which destroyed the tree. But birch body still lies near the pond.

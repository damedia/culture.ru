Проект «Образы России»

Pushkin's memorial estate (Vyazemy estate)

Eastern wing of the stable yard (wing 2)

The building was built in the late XVIII century. Small single-store wing kept the old forms of classicism better than other buildings. It has rectangular window bays with panels above the windows. Currently, it is in conservation. After the completion of restoration works the excursion department will be located here.

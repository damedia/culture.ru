The memorial estate Abramtsevo

Director's house

The house was built in 1890 of logs brought from the Vologda-Arkhangelsk railway construction. For some period of time this building was the an utility room Abramtsevo ceramic workshop. When it was moved to Moscow in 1896, estate director started to live here.

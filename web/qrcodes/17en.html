The memorial estate Abramtsevo

The workshop

Training workshop for sculpture built in 1873, designed by V. Hartman and replaced an old Aksakov's wing. The building is now a rare monument, designed by one of the most famous architects of the ""Russian style"". Richly decorated facades, wooden carvings is typical of Hartmann. According to the art historian Vladimir Stasov, the architecture of the ""Russian style"" — ""is the most important, the most talented, the most diverse, the most striking and the most graceful of all our architectures. Here in this new Russian spirit , it is much more than anything else ... Here came into play is real Russian architectural motifs, folk, one can see it in the huts, dishes, and in hundreds of household objects ... "". Mamontov, M. Antokolsky, Viktor Vasnetsov, Ilya Repin and others studied hear. It was also used as living quarters for guests coming in Abramtsevo. Valentin Serov, I. Ostroukhov, P. Konchalovsky and others stayed here .

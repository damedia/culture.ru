Pushkin's memorial estate (Vyazemy estate)

Larch

Siberian larch, pedunculate oak and lime of the regular and landscape parks are the most valuable wildlife objects, which have been preserved to present day. Larch and lime trees are the oldest (there are 16 larches on the territory). Two larchs age is dated as 190 years, they are located to the south from the palace. 170-180 years-old larches and 120-170-year-old lime trees are located in the western and north-western parts of the area. Some larchs (age of 120 to 160 years), preserved in the eastern part of the park.

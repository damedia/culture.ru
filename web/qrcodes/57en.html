Pushkin's memorial estate (Vyazemy estate)

The Dam

There was a pond with a dam on Vyazemka river, it located near its intersection with Mozhaysk road, it was 4 meters deep and had a wooden floodgate. The dam is an ancient hydroengineering construction, it was built in the time of Godunov and originally was faced with white stone. First mentioned in the documents of the XVII century. Currently, the dam has a concrete floodgate with flood-gate system. The dike is lined with trees. Today it is under reconstruction.

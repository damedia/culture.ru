The memorial estate Abramtsevo

“Russian artists in Abramtsevo in the 20 century”

The building was constructed in 1938, originally served as a sanatorium therapy center, which existed here in the 1930. It was opened to the public in 1971. Currently, there is an exhibition of works by artists of the twentieth century (Pyotr Konchalovsky, A. Lentulov, I. Mashkov, A.Osmerkin, V.Rozhdestvensky, R. Falk), as well as works by I. Grabar, D. Shmarinov, N. Andronov, A. Vasnetsov, E. Maleina, D. Shmarinov, N Udaltsova and other artists. The collection of sculptures, by Vera Mukhina, B. Korolev, S. Tavasiev, etc is hosted here. Many of these artists have lived and worked in the holiday village of artists ""New Abramtsevo"", founded in 1930. Abramtsevo estate is quite near, on the opposite bank of the river Vorya.

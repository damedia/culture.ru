Gogol alley

Named after Nikolai Gogol, 1849-1851 he visited Abramtsevo for several times in Abramtsevo. Writer loved to walk in this ally. ""Gogol took long walks with us in groves and amused himself by gathering mushrooms and leaving them to me on way back home. I could almost see how he did it. ""(ST Aksakov,"" The history of my acquaintance with Gogol ').

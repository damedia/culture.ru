Pushkin's memorial estate (Vyazemy estate)

Belfry

Belfry is located next to the church (to the north). It is made of brick with some details of white stone. Decoration of the belfry is similar to the decoration of the church. The last remaining of three similar buildings, belfry is a unique building of this type. In the middle of the XIX century monument lurched and was rectified upon E. Gendel project in 1954. Late hip roof of the building was restored. Brick wall of the church complex is a copy of the wall built in the second half of the XVIII century. It was rebuilt in the early XX century and restored in the 1980s.

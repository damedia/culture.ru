Проект «Образы России»

Pushkin's memorial estate (Vyazemy estate)

Western wing of the stable yard (wing 1)

The building was built in the late XVIII century. Construction has square shape, built of big bricks, it is typical of XVIII century. Western wing of the stable yard has not preserved the original decoration and in the 2nd half of XIX century a wooden second floor was built on it. Podsor and pritchelina (Roof edging) are decorated with carving. At present, the building houses administration and facilities services of the Museum. After the completion of restoration works here will be a children's center.
